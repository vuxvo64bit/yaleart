The School of Art galleries in Green Hall provide a year-round forum for the exhibition of work by students, faculty, and special guests in the four graduate departments of the School and the undergraduate program. 32 Edgewood Gallery is a supplemental exhibition space that is activated by School of Art students and faculty. Green Hall Gallery at 1156 Chapel Street is open to Yale community members.
