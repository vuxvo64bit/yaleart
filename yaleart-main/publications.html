    Body of Work: Yale School of Art MFA 2020

    Body of Work showcases the collected work of the graduating MFA class of 2020, and marks the fourth installment of Yale School of Art's all-school MFA book series. Featuring an afterword by Stavros Niarchos Foundation Dean and Professor of Art Marta Kuzma, and written contributions from students across all departments.

    This series of printed publications was initiated in 2017 and features the work of each recently graduated MFA class.
